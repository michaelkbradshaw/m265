Differences Between African and Asian Elephants

African Asian
Weight 4000-7500 kg 3000-6000 kg
Size and Shape of Ears Large; shape of Africa Small; shape of India
Head Shape Large; round Small; indentation in the middle
Skin Wrinkled Not wrinkled
Tusks Male and female have tusks Only males have tusks

Fun Facts About Elephants

  • An elephant drinks up to 40 gallons of water per day.
  • Elephants don't sweat! They flap their ears to cool themselves down.
  • They communicate using loud, trumpeting calls.
  • Their average lifespan is 47 years.
