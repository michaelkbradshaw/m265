Fun Facts about Chimpanzees

  • Chimpanzee live an average of 46 years.
  • They could beat humans in a wrestling match.
  • They share 98% of their DNA with humans.
  • Chimps can be aggressive and attack other groups of chimps.
  • They can learn human sign language!
