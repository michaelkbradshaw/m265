About Us

Welcome! We are a sanctuary located in Kentucky. Our primary goal is to ensure the safety and well-being of all animals that have recently been released from illegal operations, such as illegal research facilities, illegal wildlife trades, and even from immediate threats like poaching

We have been in operation for 2 years with the helpful hands of many life-long volunteers. We will open volunteer positions in the next year once we account for safety pratices in our work areas.

Location

Our zoo is located at 600 West Walnut Street in Danville, Kentucky.

Admission

There is no admission fee for our zoo! People of all ages are welcome.

Hours

Our zoo is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM every weekday.
