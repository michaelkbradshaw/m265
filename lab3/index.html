The nature of the game

In this game, you and your partner will be assessed on your knowledge of the capital of countries around the world. Each of you will have to identify the the capital of 3 countries: 3 for Player 1 and 3 for Player 2. The two of you will compete for points. Who will win?

How to play!

The questionare that you will be playing in the next few moments is a cooperative game. You and the person beside you will need to determine who is Player 1 and Player 2. By rule, Player 1 will need to be the first on the keyboard and mouse. Choose now...
