Fun Facts about Giraffes

  • A Giraffe's feet are the size of a dinner plate! (12 inches across)
  • Their caves grow 1 inch per day during their first week.
  • The record running speed of a Giraffe is 34.7 mph.
  • A herd of Girrafes is called a tower.
  • A Giraffe's spot pattern is unique; no two are alike.
